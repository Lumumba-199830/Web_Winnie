Extranet

An extranet is a private network that allows controlled access to outsiders such as business partners, suppliers, or customers. It uses internet technology but restricts access to authorized users only.

Extranets are commonly used in organizations to share important information securely. They help improve communication, speed up business processes, and allow collaboration between different companies while still maintaining privacy and security of data.

  • Controlled access to external users
  • Improves communication with partners
  • Enhances business collaboration
  • Ensures data security and privacy
  • Speeds up information sharing
