Understanding Internet and The World Wide Web

What you need to know about the Internet.

The Internet and the World Wide Web play a vital role in modern communication and information sharing. This website introduces key concepts that explain how digital systems connect and interact, including technologies such as the World Wide Web (WWW), URLs, and TCP/IP. It also explores different types of networks like intranets and extranets, showing how they are used in real-world environments. Through this, users can gain a better understanding of how the Internet works and supports everyday online activities.
